Glitch Images

Collection of liminal spaces, glitched to become even more unsettling.

Liminality is defined as a state of transition from one stage to another,
often likened to a rite of passage, but liminality can also be observed in architecture,
which are what these images focus on, being considered odd spaces that are not meant to be occupied,
but only passed through.
